# Navy + Gold Palette — Plumbing / Trades
**Date:** 2026-03-29
**Category:** design
**Industry:** Plumbing, HVAC, trades

## Colors
- Primary: Navy #1a2744
- Accent: Gold #d4a853
- Gold dark (WCAG safe): #8a6f2f
- Background: White + #f8f9fa (gray-50)
- Footer: #111c30

## Why It Works
- Navy = trust, reliability, professionalism
- Gold = premium, established, quality
- Strong contrast between the two
- Reads masculine without being aggressive
- Differentiated from the typical "blue + white" plumber template

## WCAG Notes
- Gold #d4a853 on white FAILS AA for small text (3.2:1)
- Gold dark #8a6f2f on white PASSES AA (5.2:1)
- Use gold-dark for any text on white backgrounds
- Gold on navy is fine for large text (headings)

## Takeaway
Use navy + gold for any trade business wanting to look established. Always use the darker gold variant for text on white.
